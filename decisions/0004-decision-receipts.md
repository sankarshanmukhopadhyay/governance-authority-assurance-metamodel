# ADR 0004: 0004 Decision Receipts

**Status:** Accepted for v0.5.0

## Decision

Decision receipts are mandatory for consequential effects and profile-defined boundary events.

## Assurance consequence

Conformance evidence and tests SHALL reflect this decision.
