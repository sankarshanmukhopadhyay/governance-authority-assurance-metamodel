The [Governance, Authority and Assurance Metamodel v0.9.0](governance-authority-assurance-metamodel.md) is the authoritative human-readable specification.

## Publication model

- Human-readable normative semantics are defined by the specification.
- JSON Schemas are normative for artifact structure.
- Controlled vocabularies govern enumerated semantic values.
- Profiles define composable conformance targets.
- Executable tests provide repository-generated evidence, not independent assessment.
