# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Contributors and maintainers commit to a respectful, inclusive and technically rigorous environment. Participation must remain free from harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory conduct and bad-faith disruption.

## Expected behaviour

- Critique ideas, requirements and evidence rather than people.
- State assumptions and disclose material conflicts of interest.
- Distinguish normative defects from implementation preferences.
- Give appropriate attribution and preserve contributor provenance.
- Treat security, safety and affected-party concerns seriously.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation where conduct threatens the integrity or safety of the project. Reports may be submitted privately through the process described in `SECURITY.md` when public disclosure would create risk.
