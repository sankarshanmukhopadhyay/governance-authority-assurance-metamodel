# Specification Roadmap

## v0.9.0 completion state

The candidate normative surface, eight profiles, canonical schemas, governed vocabularies, behavioural vectors, threat traceability, CI validation and reproducible package are present.

## v1.0.0 readiness programme

1. Obtain two independent implementation reports covering Foundation and at least one composed profile.
2. Resolve all critical and high candidate issues.
3. Demonstrate canonical schema publication and package retrieval.
4. Validate cross-implementation decision receipts and lifecycle events.
5. Complete privacy, affected-party and security review.
6. Approve the stable-release governance decision.
